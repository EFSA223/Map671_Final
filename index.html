Tornadoes in the United States

Authored by Etienne Sambo

This is the final assignment for the "Online Graduate Education in Digital Mapping" at the Univerity of Kentucky.
Map671-202, "Introduction to New Mapping", jun 2018.

About the Map:
This Map is composed by counting the number of all tornadoes per county and categorized according to the "Fujita scale" (F0 - F5).

Application used: 64 bit QGIS 2.18.18 on Windows 10 and CARTO.

Data source: NOAA's National Weather Service. "Storm Prediction Center", Data CRS: WGS84 (EPSG 4269)
